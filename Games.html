Thor's Thunder:

Reaction time/hand eye coordination game wherein you must click the lighting bolts that appear on the screen before the timer runs out.

There are 4 modes of difficulty: Easy, Medium, Hard, and Nightmare.
Each mode increases speed and frequency of the lightning bolts. However, each mode also decreases the time limit.
